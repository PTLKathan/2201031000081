Feature

Automated Calls

Using advanced image 
recognition technology, the 
AI umpire could make real-
time decisions on balls, 
strikes, or outs in baseball, 
or LBW (leg before wicket) 
decisions in cricket.

Consistency

The AI umpire would 
make calls 
consistently 
throughout the game, 
avoiding human error 
or bias that can occur 
with human umpires.

Instant Replay

The AI umpire 
could quickly 
review contentious 
calls and provide 
instant feedback, 
reducing delays 
in the game.

Rule Adherence

It would ensure that 
all calls are made in 
accordance with the 
rules of the game, 
eliminating 
subjective 
interpretation.

Player Tracking

Through player tracking 
technology, the AI 
umpire could accurately 
determine if a runner is 
safe or out in baseball or 
if a batsman has crossed 
the crease in cricket.

Statistical Analysis

The AI umpire could provide 
detailed statistical analysis 
of the game, including 
strike zones, batting 
averages, and other 
metrics, helping teams 
improve their performance.

Language Support

Multilingual support could 
be incorporated to 
communicate decisions 
effectively in different 
languages, making the 
game accessible to a wider 
audience.

Training Tool

It could serve as a training 
tool for players, coaches, 
and officials, providing 
insights into the nuances of 
the game and helping 
them improve their 
understanding of the rules.
